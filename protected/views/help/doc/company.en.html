Company data entry form
Here is where you enter your companies.  Most users will have only one company to work with and in such a case then there should be only one entry made with this form.  Also, you need to then go to  OPTIONS->SETUP  for more information on how to choose a default company.

Company number

This is an integer number that can be whatever you like but it must be uniq.  That is, you may not have two companies with the same numbers.

Company name

This can be any information you want.  Usually it is the company name.
