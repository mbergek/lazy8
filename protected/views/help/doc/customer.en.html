Customer/Project data entry form
Here is where you enter your Customer/Projects names.   However, you might need to do some thing before you begin entering accounts here.  If the company you want to enter accounts on does not show up on the form, you must go to another form and enter the company number and name you are going to work with.  If this is your case, Go to the menu DataEntry->Company

This is not required information and this program will work fine without it.  But, if you use and enter this information, you can get some important statistics on specific Customer/Projects and how they effect your company

Company

Choose here which company you want to work with.  Most users will have only one company to work with and in such a case then there should be no selection necessary and the company will come here automatically and in such a case this field will even be disabled so you wont be able to choose a company.  Please see SETUP  for more information on how to choose a default company and Company to see how to enter more companies.
 

Customer/Project number

This is an integer number that can be whatever you want but this number must be uniq for just this account you are entering for this particular company. For your convience, this number is auto-incremented so that you know what is the next available number. But, you don't have to use the suggested number and are encouraged to use your own numbering system.

Customer/Project name

This can be any information you want.  It usually is the Customer/Projects name.

Customer/Project description

This can be any information you want.  Usually it appropriately describes the Customer/Project

Default account

This "Default Account" will be automatically added to the Transaction Form when a Customer/Project is selected and no account is already entered into the Transaction Form for that row. .
