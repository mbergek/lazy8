Welcome to Lazy 8 Ledger 2.24
You should allow this help window to remain visible untill you get more aquainted with Lazy 8 Ledger. This window gives you important information about each field you enter information into. Text reports will appear in the empty editor window in the center area under different tabs. You may also use this center area for writing, saving and opening notes and files. The windows in this program are seperated by bars that you can click on and move. There is also an option to have all windows floating seperately if you desire
For general information about this product, click your mouse on the blue text 'general information.' You can click on most any blue text you see in this help window to get more information on that subject. If this window seems too small for some information you see, grab the seperator bar with the mouse and drag open the window as much as you need.
