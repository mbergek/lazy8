The Lazy8 Ledger Tutorial

Lesson: The general work flow

Mostly, you will be using the Transaction data entry form, but before you can get there, you must make sure everything is in order..


This will help you get going as quickly as possible.  Follow the steps and you will soon be doing well.

1. The first time you start Lazy8 Ledger, you should be prompted to enter a Company, Accounts, etc. If you didnt do this process, then go to the menu ADVANCED-CREATE A NEW COMPANY. After you do this, you can then proceed with step 4 below. If you want instead to do everything manually, do steps 2 to 3 first and then continue with step 4.
2.  After installation, Enter your company name.  see the menu DataEntry->Company
3.  Enter your accounts.  see the menu DataEntry->Account As an alternative to entering all your own accounts, you can use a predefined group of accounts that follow the EU BAS 2003 accounting system that comes with this program. These predefined accounts represent the most commonly used accounts for small businesses. To load up these predefined accounts, choose the menu Advanced->CREATE A NEW COMPANY.
4.  Enter your Customer/Projects.  This is optional.  Most wont need to do this but if you want to have special statistics on your specific Customer/Projects, then .  see the menu DataEntry->Customer/Project.
5.  Enter your invoices.  See the menu DataEntry->Transaction.
6.  Print out all you have done.  This of course is optional but it is also really fun.  Go to the menu Report and have fun with all the reports.
7.  Backup what you have done.  This is optional but highly suggested. Advanced->Backup/restore->backup database
